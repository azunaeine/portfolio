Bio

My name is Azuna Ekundayo. I was born in Benin city, Nigeria, and I was raised in Edmonton, Alberta. I first gained an interest in coding when I was younger. However, as I got older I discovered UX design. UX Design is a field that I am immensely interested in as it incorporates psychology, design thinking, and design. I currently reside in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. In my first year of college, I interned at multiple companies. I interned at Brainracers, Sellynow, Turnup Activism, Crush Burnout, Ayana Therapy, and Power Changes Lives. I took on different roles in these internships. Some of them involved design. The others involved product or project management and UX research.
