UI/UX Design

Magnitude

Online dating has been a very popular option for many as it allows people to expand their range when it comes to finding potential soulmates. However, the presence of online dating has limitations and problems when it comes to displaying intimacy and engaging with your potential match. Magnitude is an AI dating app that aims to enrich peoples dating experience by providing them with various ways to engage with their matches virtually.

Role

  • User Research,
  • Visual Design,
  • UX Design

Tools

  • Figma

Timeline

  • 10 weeks

Team

  • Azuna Ekundayo
  • Emeka Agara

Prototype

Problem

Intimacy and engagement in a relationship are important. Potential matches in online dating apps find it difficult to mingle and interact with one another and often have to go to public places to interact with their partners.

Challenge

How might we develop an AI dating app that improves people's dating experience when it comes to intimacy and engagement with their matches virtually?

Solution

Magnitude is an AI dating app that provides people with a unique experience because people are able to search for nearby users around them, identify users with the use of their camera, and connect with them with the potential to go on a virtual date.
