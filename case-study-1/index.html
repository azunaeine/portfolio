UI/UX Design

Magnitude

Online dating has been a very popular option for many as it allows people to expand their range when it comes to finding potential soulmates. However, the presence of online dating has limitations and problems when it comes to displaying intimacy and engaging with your potential match virtually. Magnitude is an AI dating app that aims to improve people's dating experience by providing them with various ways to engage with their matches virtually.

Role

  • User Research,
  • Visual Design,
  • UX Design

Tools

  • Figma

Timeline

  • 10 weeks

Team

  • Azuna Ekundayo
  • Emeka Agara

Prototype

Problem

Intimacy and engagement in a relationship are important. Potential matches in online dating apps find it difficult to mingle and interact with one another virtually and often have to go to public places to interact with their partners from these online dating apps.

Challenge

How might we develop an AI dating app that improves people's dating experience when it comes to intimacy and engagement with their matches virtually?

Solution

Magnitude is an AI dating app that provides people with a unique experience because people are able to search for nearby users around them, identify users with the use of their camera, and connect with them with the potential to go on a virtual date.



Location

Helps users find nearby users and potential matches through the map.

Facial Recognition

Magnitude possesses a facial recognition feature on its app that helps people identify users through the lenses of their camera.

Messaging

This enables potential matches in the AI dating app to message each other.

Process

User Research

Interviews

In order to understand people's online dating experience in a better perspective and to find ways to improve their dating experience virtually, me and my partner conducted multiple user interviews that involved people who were in a long-term relationship. Our primary aim for interviewing came when analyzing and assessing three questions:

  1. How did they display intimacy when on dating apps?
  2. How often did they use dating apps?
  3. Why do they use dating apps?

Insights

The participants in the interviews gave us shocking responses. Some of the participants in the interviews stated that there weren't any AI dating apps available or any dating apps that helped them go on virtual dates. In addition, they claimed that most mobile applications lacked the technology to include virtual dates in their platform. Furthermore, the participants also said that many dating apps lacked features that would help them be intimate with their matches virtually. Me and my partner acquired feedback and insights about the most desirable features they would like to have in an AI dating app:

Analyzing the Data

Me and my partner kept thinking of ways to connect the users experience and the app’s value. We continued to ponder and think and we began to analyze the data. We came up with a venn diagram to connect our ideas.

Design

Brainstorming

The insights that we collected from the interviews helped us improve our features in our application. Furthermore, these insights we collected from the interviews helped us identify weak spots in our application. I created a diagram comparing the insights from the interviews to the features we wanted to use to address the user’s needs.

Wireframe

Me and my teammate started creating low fidelity wireframes to help us have a visual idea of what we wanted the mobile application to look like. In addition, we created low fidelity wireframes to have an idea of the features we wanted to prioritize in our application.

Visual System

We completed our wireframes and we were satisfied with our mobile application's look and structure. From there, we began to think about the visual system. Our primary aim was to use a colour palette that was simple and not too strong.

High Fidelity Design

The high fidelity design helped me and my partner have a good idea of what our mobile application would like with the presence of colours. I also wanted to test for usability so I wanted my high fidelity design to be completely polished and look good.

Usability Test

When I reached this stage, I started to assess and look at some of the screens I had created and I wanted to test them with a diverse set of users. So I decided to test the prototype with two participants ranging from a dating coach to a married couple. Here is what I did during the usability testing:

  1. Introduce the premise of the project
  2. Describe the circumstances of the task
  3. Ask any questions regarding the design of the screens and obtain feedback from testing

Reflection

What I Learned

Patience: I learned to be patient, especially in the design process. I often realized when designing that I would rush and design screens that did not look visually appealing or good.
Confidence: I also learned that I must be confident when designing. Never look down on yourself.
