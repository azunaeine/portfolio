UI/UX Design

Muna

Video streaming enables people to watch video content without turning on their television. It has provided people with flexibility and a way to access video content from any location besides their living room or any room with a television. However, many video streaming mobile applications struggle to create a public space of interaction between users. People are not able to interact or share their opinions about certain movies and tv shows in an online video streaming mobile application as this feature does not exist in many video streaming mobile applications.

Role

  • User Research,
  • Visual Design,
  • UX Design

Tools

  • Figma

Timeline

  • 18 weeks

Team

  • Azuna Ekundayo

Prototype

Problem

Video streaming has been a demanding platform for many years. However, many video streaming apps possess many flaws as they fail to connect the experience of social media and video streaming. Muna is a video streaming platform that aims to connect a community of users and offer the experience of video streaming.

Challenge

How do I design a video streaming app that bridges the gap between social media and video streaming?

Solution

In response to the problem, I developed a mobile application called Muna. Muna is a video streaming app that bridges the gap between social media and video streaming. Muna does this by displaying some unique features that are quite prevalent in some social media apps. These features present in Muna are newsfeed, messages and groups. In addition, these features provide an enhanced virtual experience for the user with the presence of video streaming on the platform.



Chatting

This allows users to chat with other users on the mobile application.

Video streaming

This allows users to stream movies and videos while actively interacting with other users on that mobile application page.

Groups

This gives users the freedom to access communities in the mobile application platform and engage with other users by sharing posts related to that community.

Process

User Research

Competitive Analysis

I looked at other mobile applications that promoted or utilized video streaming. I analyzed each application and looked at their pros and cons. In addition, I listed the pros and cons of each video streaming app. While doing this, I was able to have a better understanding of the problem at hand.

Personas

Since working adults were most likely to spend more money than students. I used the results from the interviews I conducted and created one persona reflecting a working adult. This helped to guide my design process.

Brainstorming

I utilized the personas to create features that I wanted to address and implement in the mobile application. I noticed in my competitor analysis that there weren't any mobile applications that incorporated video streaming and interactivity with other users on the platform. I began to ask myself a question after reviewing my competitor analysis, how do I design a video streaming app that bridges the gap between social media and video streaming?

Wireframe

I created low-fidelity wireframes to help me have a visual idea of what I wanted the mobile application to look like. In addition, the low-fidelity wireframe helped me organize all my design ideas.

Visual System

I completed the low-fidelity wireframe and I was happy with the mobile application's look. From there, I proceeded with the visual system. The visual system helped me think of the colours to add to the mobile application.

High Fidelity Design

The high-fidelity design helped me have a good idea of what my mobile application would look like with the presence of colours.

Reflection

Overall, I was proud of Muna. I felt that Muna was the solution to the challenge. Muna is the perfect bridge between video streaming and interactivity. Muna defies other video streaming applications that are current in today's market.
